  • Elephant

    Name: Loxodonta Africana & Elephas Maximus
    Maximum Age: 70
    Maximum height: 4 m (13 ft.)
    Maximum Weight: 7,000 kg
    Food: Herbivorous
    Where:They live throughout the sub-Sahara Africa, South Asia, and Southeast Asia. Fact: Elephants live in family groups, which can consist of one female with her calves or several related females with offspring.
